Manage Your Posts Only Plugin

The Manage/View your Posts only plugin developed by Zibeb Tips and Reviews is a very simple yet useful plugin that allows an administrator to let a contributor see only the posts that they have submitted or written in draft form without allowing them to view the posts written by others in Wordpress. By default, wordpress lets a contributor see the titles of the posts to other contributors as well. While this may not be a big deal for many bloggers, it may pose some trouble/issues for some.
